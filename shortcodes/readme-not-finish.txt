**Only works with multisite installation!**

= Why is not working in my WordPress site or WordPress multisite installation? =

Extensions for PressBooks is an add-on of PressBooks. Without PressBooks, will not work.

= Why is not working with my theme? =

Extensions for PressBooks is created to be used with our child theme. But any child theme could work with little modifications.
